Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I get in touch with you?

    A. You can click here to submit your event details or call us on 886-105-3650.

  • Are the rates mentioned in the website final prices?

    A. The rates mentioned on the websites are rack rates and are usually negotiable.We provide you the final lowest prices, based on your requirements and the availability of the venue on your desired date, once you create your event and shortlist the venues/vendors.To send us an enquiry, you can click here or call 90-459-222-55.

  • Are you an event planner?

    A. No, we do not plan events. Instead, we empower you to plan your event hassle-free through the use of technology, comprehensive and current information of venues and vendors.

  • How much do you charge from customers for your service?

    A. Our service is free of costs. We do not charge our customers. We get paid by the venue owners for listing and marketing their business.

  • What kind of events do you cater to?

    A. You name it, we have it!

  • My venue is finalised, how do I pay the booking amount?

    A. You can either pay the booking amount to the venue, seems more convenient to you. Once you have paid the booking amount, your booking on the selected date is 100% confirmed!

  • How can I make changes or cancel my booking?

    A. The changes/cancellations can be made according to the terms and conditions of the venue you have booked.

  • When can we contact you?

    A. We offer a dedicated support service to our customers. Our event experts are available from 9 am to 9 pm every day at 886-105-3650, in case you have any urgent requirements or if you are facing any issues with your booking.
